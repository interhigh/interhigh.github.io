Youth Worship Service
Every Sunday, InterHigh volunteers come to your church to provide a Sunday program for middle and high school youth. This includes relatable messages, small group time and activities.
InterHigh Monthly
Once a month, students from the different InterHigh churches get together for a one day outing, where they have the chance to bond with Christian students from other churches.
Retreats
Twice a year, we gather the youths from all the different churches together for a retreat. It’s a time of biblical and relevant messages, fellowship, studying God’s word, and having fun together.
Worldview Camps
Interhigh provides a one day training camp that is open to all youth groups to train and equip your students in apologetics and Christian living.
